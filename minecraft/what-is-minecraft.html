What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a single or multiplayer sandbox survival game that allows players to explore endless different worlds. Players are given the freedom to build and create their own structures, mine for resources, and craft tools and items to aid in their survival.

Click here to try a very early version of Minecraft



Since its release, it has changed the 3D game industry significantly. Surpassing 300 million sales as of 2024, it remains one of the most popular games ever made.
