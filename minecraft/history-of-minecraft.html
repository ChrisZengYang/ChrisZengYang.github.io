The History of Minecraft

Released in 2011 by Mojang Studios, Minecraft was created by Markus "Notch" Persson. Development began in May 17, 2009, but was originally made as an experiment to test random cave generation.

Looking at Minecraft's early development, you'd probably be shocked at how simple it was. The graphics were simple and way less detailed, and the gameplay was very basic.

Development

At first, Notch worked on Minecraft by himself. Other people started to work on it with Notch after he started a company named Mojang Specifications, later renamed to simply Mojang.

Jens "Jeb" Bergensten, lead developer of Minecraft

The game was released on October 7th, 2011, with a mobile version following shortly after. Later, Notch sold Minecraft to Microsoft in 2014 for $2.5 Billion USD.
Over the years, Minecraft has made its way to numerous different platforms, including:

  • PC (Windows)
  • Xbox One and 360
  • Nintendo Switch
  • PlayStation
  • iOS
