The History of Minecraft

Back

Released in 2011 by Mojang Studios, Minecraft was created by Markus "Notch" Persson. Development began in May 17, 2009, but was originally made as an experiment to test random cave generation.

Looking at Minecraft's early development, you'd probably be shocked at how simple it was. The graphics were simple and way more saturated, and the gameplay was very basic.
